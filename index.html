Leonid
Foundation

Founded in 2022 in New Jersey by Anna and Edward Murov — in the first days after Russia's full-scale invasion. It began with a single Facebook post and a synagogue filled with boxes. Today it is a stable humanitarian, cultural and advocacy platform.

Their focus: children, women, and mothers affected by war in Ukraine. Aid shipments, refugee resettlement, legal support, trauma therapy for children, and advocacy in Washington, D.C.

Our Philosophy

"We decided to create something that helps you give from fullness — because only from fullness do we create a better world."

Each person passes on their state and improves the life of another — like links in a chain. This is how the world can climb out of where we currently live. One nourished person at a time.
